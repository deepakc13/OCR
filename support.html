Getting Started

How to Use the OCR Service

  1. Upload Your Document: Click the “Upload” button on the homepage. Supported file format JPG.
  2. Process the Document: After uploading, click the “Process” button to begin extracting text from your image or document.
  3. Download Your Results: Once the processing is complete, download the extracted text in your preferred format (TXT, DOCX, or PDF).

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Poor Text Recognition: Ensure the document is clear and has good contrast. Handwritten text may have lower accuracy.
  • File Upload Issues: Ensure the file is in a supported format (JPG) and is under 10 MB.
  • Slow Processing Time: Large documents may take longer to process. Try uploading smaller files for faster results.
  • Unable to Download Results: Check your internet connection and ensure pop-up blockers are disabled in your browser.

How to Improve OCR Accuracy

  • High-Quality Scans: Scan documents in high resolution .
  • Straighten the Document: Avoid skewed images for better text extraction.
  • Text Orientation: If the document is rotated, use the "Rotate" option before uploading.

FAQ

Q: How accurate is your OCR service?

A: Our OCR engine is highly accurate for printed text, but recognition accuracy may vary based on document quality, handwriting, and font style.

Q: Can I OCR scanned images of handwritten text?

A: Handwritten text may not be as accurately recognized as printed text. The legibility of handwriting can impact the result.

Q: What file formats are supported?

A: We support PDF, PNG, and JPG formats. We are continuously working to add more formats.
